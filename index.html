Meet the Bladers: Valt

Valt is a cheerful and energetic boy who is very passionate about Beyblade. He has a strong bond with his Bey Valtryek, and this relationship is one of the factors that motivates him to become a better Blader. Even though he can be a little over-enthusiastic at times, he manages to become friends with anyone as if he has known them for years.

Featured Characters

Meet the Bladers: Rantaro

Rantaro is a passionate Blader who wants to be seen as a tough guy, but is just a really big softie. He never hesitates to express himself, and is a genuinely honest person through and through. Though he tries not to let on, he cares a lot about Valt and his growth as a Blader which makes him one of Valt’s biggest cheerleaders and closest friends.

Featured Characters

Meet the Bladers: Shu

Shu is Valt’s best friend, and as a member of the Supreme Four, he is considered to be a Beyblade prodigy. Cool, calm, and collected, Shu works extremely hard to be the best Blader he can be. His relationship with Valt, a unique combination of friendship and rivalry, motivates them both to continuously improve and grow together.

Meet the Bladers: Daigo

Daigo is a very competitive Blader who is passionate about winning. He is a talented strategist, and is great at analyzing battles and predicting his opponents’ moves. He has a cold and distant personality, but begins to open up when battling with his Beigoma Club friends. Battling with them makes him remember how much fun he has while playing the sport.

Meet the Bladers: Xander

Trained in martial arts from a young age by his family, Xander is an extremely strong Blader with incredible discipline. He is the generous and kind leader of the Swordflames Beyblade Club, and a member of the Supreme Four. He is a respected competitor who is confident in his abilities as both a Blader and a leader.

Meet the Bladers: Wakiya

Wakiya is an extremely smart and talented Blader who is confident that no one can beat him. He is widely acknowledged as a proficient Blader with high aspirations. He is very diligent and trains whenever he has a free moment, because for him, BEYBLADE is far from just a game.

Meet the Bladers: Zac

Zac, a member of the Supreme Four, is a beloved pop star who goes by the alias “Zac the Sunrise.” He has a bright and enthusiastic personality that lights up the room. Zac is a born entertainer who loves Beyblade as much as he loves the spotlight.

Meet the Bladers: Lui

Lui is an aloof genius, whose overwhelming power has led him to victory in four consecutive National Tournament championships. His disregard for the feelings of other Bladers creates friction with even his own teammates. As the strongest of the Supreme Four, he only battles opponents he thinks are worthy. If a battle isn’t a challenge, Lui wants no part in it.
